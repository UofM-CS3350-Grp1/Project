
Must be done after the automated tests

Manual test to see that a service can be deleted.

1. Open the program.
2. Login using a correct user name and password
3. Click the menu button
4. Select Services from the drop down menu
5. Verify that a Service called "Test Service" exists
6. Select the Service "Test Service"
7. Click the Cancel Service button
8. Click yes on the message box that appears
9. Verify that the Service called "Test Service" no longer exists
