
Must be done after the automated tests

Manual test to see that a client can be deleted.

1. Open the program.
2. Login using a correct user name and password
3. Click the menu button
4. Select Clients from the drop down menu
5. Verify that a Client called "Test Business Name" exists
6. Select the Client "Test Business Name"
7. Click the Cancel Client Button
8. Click yes on the message box that appears
9. Verify that the Client called "Test Business Name" no longer exists
